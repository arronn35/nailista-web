Questions

Answered.

When does Nailista launch?+

Nailista is now available on the App Store and ready for users. You can access the app by clicking the “Available on App Store” button.

Is Nailista free to use?+

The core app — social feed, discovery, messaging — is free. AI design generations use credits; waitlist members start with 500 free.

How does the AI design work?+

Tell Nailista what you want (in words or with a reference photo), choose a shape, length and finish, and it renders photoreal nail designs in seconds.

Can I book appointments through the app?+

Yes. Message any artist, send an appointment request, and track everything in your Inbox — no more hopping between DMs, texts and spreadsheets.

I own a salon. How do I join?+

Sign up for the waitlist and select “business” when we reach out. You’ll get a verified profile, portfolio showcase, and booking management tools.

Which platforms is Nailista on?+

Launching on iOS first. Android follows shortly after.
